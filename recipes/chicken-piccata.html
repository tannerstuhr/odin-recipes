Easy Chicken Piccata

Description:

A delicious and nutritious chicken recipe that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large chicken breasts
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 stick butter
  • salt and pepper
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 3 medium lemons
  • 2 tbsp drained capers
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
